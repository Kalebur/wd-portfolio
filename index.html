About Me

I fell in love with technology as a little boy and got my first experience with programming using Visual Basic 6 during the early 2000's. After that, I've been tinkering with various languages and technologies until finally settling on web development a couple of years ago. I've been studying web development since and am looking forward to making a career out of it!

Outside of my interest in web dev, I'm into video games, game development and 3D art. I've spent some time working with the Unity game engine and am going to be looking into the Unreal engine as well.
